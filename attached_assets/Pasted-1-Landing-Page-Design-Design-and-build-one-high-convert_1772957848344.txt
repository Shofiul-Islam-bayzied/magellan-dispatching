1. Landing Page Design



Design and build one high-converting landing page.



The page should look:



• Modern
• Professional
• Trustworthy
• Optimized for mobile



The majority of drivers will visit from mobile devices, so mobile optimization is critical.



Images should include:



• Semi trucks
• Truck drivers
• Logistics / dispatch visuals



2. Landing Page Structure



The page should include the following sections:



Hero Section



Headline example:



Get a Dedicated Dispatcher and Higher Paying Loads



Subheadline explaining the service clearly.



Trust badges such as:



• Dedicated Dispatcher
• No Forced Dispatch
• 24/7 Driver Support
• Keep More of Your Revenue



Primary call-to-action button throughout the page:



Check Eligibility



This button should lead to the booking calendar.



Driver Problems Section



Explain common problems truck drivers face:



• Spending hours searching load boards
• Brokers offering low rates
• Too much paperwork
• No time to focus on driving



Our Dispatch Solution



Explain how the dispatch service works and how it helps drivers.



Examples:



• Load sourcing
• Rate negotiation
• Broker credit checks
• Paperwork management
• Route planning



Benefits Section



Explain why drivers should work with us.



Examples:



• Higher paying loads
• Dedicated dispatcher
• No forced dispatch
• Paperwork handled
• Focus on driving



How It Works



Simple 3-step process:



Step 1 — Apply
Step 2 — Speak with our dispatch team
Step 3 — Start receiving better loads



Explainer Video Section



The page should include space for a 60–90 second explainer video that will be placed on the page.



Booking Section



Embed a Calendly booking calendar where drivers can schedule a call.



3. Lead Capture & Booking Integration



Set up the booking system so that:



• Drivers can schedule a call through Calendly
• Booking confirmations are sent automatically
• Email notifications are triggered when a booking is made



4. Tracking Setup



Basic tracking should be installed:



• Meta Pixel
• Basic analytics tracking



This allows us to track ad performance.
